# How to Close More of the Plumbing Leads You Already Get in Brisbane

Most Brisbane plumbers think their revenue problem is a lead problem. It isn't.

You're already getting calls. You're already getting form fills. The problem is what happens — or doesn't happen — in the 20 minutes after that lead comes in. That gap is where revenue disappears.

---

## The Real Problem: You're Not Short on Leads. You're Short on Conversion.

Think about the last week of enquiries you received. How many of those jobs did you actually close?

For most Brisbane plumbing businesses, the answer is far lower than it should be. Not because the leads were bad. Because the follow-up was slow, incomplete, or never happened.

Industry surveys consistently show that 78% of buyers hire the first business to respond. In Brisbane, where a homeowner shopping for a plumber will call two or three businesses in the same sitting, speed is the entire game. Response time data from industry research shows conversion risk increases 10x at a 5-minute delay and 100x at 10 minutes.

You're not losing those jobs to a better plumber. You're losing them to a faster phone.

---

## Why the Obvious Fix Doesn't Work

The instinct is to hire someone to answer the phones or send out a follow-up. But that creates its own problems.

A receptionist costs money every hour, including the hours when call volume is low. They knock off at 5pm. They get sick. And when a lead comes in at 7pm on a Wednesday — which Brisbane homeowners absolutely do — nobody picks up.

Sending follow-ups manually means whoever is responsible for the job (usually you) has to remember to do it, find time to do it, and track which quotes are still outstanding. When you're running a crew, that's the first thing to fall through the cracks.

The result: most plumbing businesses in Brisbane are running at 30-40% quote close rates when they could be running at 50-60%.

---

## The Three Levers That Actually Move the Number

There are three specific points in the lead-to-job journey where Brisbane plumbers lose revenue. Fix all three and the close rate shifts materially.

**Lever 1: First-response speed**

The moment a call goes unanswered or a form fill sits in your inbox, the clock starts. An AI receptionist answers every missed call within 5 minutes with a personalised SMS — acknowledging the enquiry, confirming someone will be in touch, and gathering job details automatically.

That 5-minute touch doesn't just keep the lead warm. It signals professionalism before the competition has even called back.

**Lever 2: Automated quote follow-up**

Industry data shows that 60% or more of quotes convert after a follow-up. Most plumbing businesses send exactly zero follow-ups. The quote goes out and then nothing — until the homeowner books someone else.

A structured SMS sequence changes this. Day 1 after the quote: a short check-in. Day 3: an objection-handling message that keeps the conversation open. Day 7 and Day 14: a final nudge for homeowners who are still deciding or waiting for competing quotes to come in.

This sequence runs automatically. You don't have to remember. The system tracks when each quote was sent and fires the right message at the right time.

**Lever 3: client management system (CRM) pipeline visibility**

If you don't know which quotes are outstanding right now, you can't manage them. our client management platform, configured for plumbing businesses, gives you a live view of every lead and quote in your pipeline — where each one sits, how long it's been sitting, and what the next action is.

No more relying on memory or a spreadsheet you haven't updated since Tuesday.
