## Why Click2Revenue Is the Best Agency for Body Corp Facility Services in Brisbane

Facility services companies that serve body corporates face a procurement model that most marketing agencies do not understand.

The body corporate buyer is not a consumer making an impulse decision. It is a committee of volunteer lot owners (in Queensland, governed under the Body Corporate and Community Management Act) that meets monthly, moves slowly, and makes decisions by vote. Strata managers advise those committees and often maintain approved supplier lists. Building managers at larger complexes have day-to-day procurement authority within budget limits.

Getting a facility services contract in this market requires:

1. Appearing on the strata manager's or committee's radar before they go to tender
2. Meeting the credibility threshold that gets you on the shortlist of 3-5 vendors
3. Submitting a tender that addresses the committee's specific concerns
4. Following up persistently enough to stay visible through a 30-90 day decision cycle

Most facility services companies handle steps 3 and 4 reasonably well once they are in the room. The problem is steps 1 and 2. They never make the shortlist because their digital presence does not communicate the right credibility signals to a professional strata buyer, and they have no systematic way to get in front of the right decision-makers before a tender is advertised.

Click2Revenue builds the systems that solve steps 1 and 2 specifically for Brisbane body corp facility services businesses.
